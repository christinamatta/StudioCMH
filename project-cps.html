Architectural Renovation — Finishes, Kitchen & Bathroom Design
Selected work completed while at a previous firm; role indicated for clarity. Imagery credited where applicable.

A complete architectural renovation within Central Park Tower — the world's tallest residential building — encompassing the full selection of architectural finishes alongside the design and specification of the kitchens and bathrooms throughout.

The approach was guided by restraint and precision: materials were chosen for their quality and quietude, allowing the extraordinary views and the apartment's proportions to remain the primary experience. Clean lines, warm wood tones, and considered stone selections give the residence a calm, sophisticated character that endures.
