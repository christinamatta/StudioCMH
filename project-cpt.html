Located within the world's tallest residential tower, this Central Park Tower renovation focused on a complete reimagining of the kitchen and living areas — driven by the client's desire to create moments of dramatic display.

A custom glass vitrine was created to showcase curated china and wine, functioning as both bespoke storage and a defining focal point. Anchoring the living area, a sculptural chandelier was positioned at the center of the plan, reinforcing a quiet theatricality that plays against the tower's panoramic sky-high views.

Dark, richly figured marbles — notably a dramatic black-and-gold stone in the bathrooms — set a bold counterpoint to the luminous, sky-filled living spaces, creating a dialogue between intimacy and grandeur throughout the apartment.
