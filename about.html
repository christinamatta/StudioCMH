About

Studio
CMH

Studio CMH is an interior designer firm based in New York City, specializing in high-end residential and luxury development. Offering full-suite design service, from concept through design development to completion.

With a refined, detail-driven approach, each project is guided by a thoughtful and collaborative design process.

The result is work that is elegant and timeless — creating spaces that feel tailored, polished, yet deeply personal.

Get in touch
Design Philosophy
"Each project begins with listening — to the client, to the space. What emerges is always singular: a space that carries the mark of its inhabitants, refined through a process of reiteration."
Services

Residential Design

Full-service interior design for private residences — from space planning and material specification to furniture selection and final styling.

Luxury Development

Design of model units, amenity spaces, lobbies, and common areas for high-end residential and mixed-use developments. Translating developer vision into compelling, market-ready interiors.

Design Consultation

Design direction and advisory for clients who require expert guidance on material palettes, spatial planning, or concept development at any stage of a project.
