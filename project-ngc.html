Selected work completed while at a previous firm.

A quiet, artful retreat that adapts effortlessly between private living and meaningful moments of connection.

At the heart of the project is the clients' extensive art collection, which guided the material palette. Walls were treated as restrained backdrops, allowing each piece to be experienced with clarity and intention, while subtle layers of texture and warmth introduce depth without competing with the artwork.

Entertaining remains an integral part of the clients' lives. Flexible seating arrangements, a thoughtfully composed dining setting, and seamless transitions between spaces allow the home to accommodate gatherings with ease, while maintaining a sense of intimacy for day-to-day living.

The result is a residence that feels both elevated and deeply personal — a carefully curated home designed equally for hosting and for living.
